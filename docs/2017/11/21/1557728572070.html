1、多个Filter的执行顺序

  1. <filter> 
    2.  
    
  2.         <filter-name>MyServlet1</filter-name> 
    3.  
    
  3.         <filter-class>com.alibaba.exchange.MyServlet1</filter-class> 
    4.  
    
  4.     </filter> 
    5.  
    
  5.     <filter-mapping> 
    6.  
    
  6.         <filter-name>MyServlet1</filter-name> 
    7.  
    
  7.         <url-pattern>/*</url-pattern> 
    8.  
    
  8.     </filter-mapping> 
    9.  
    
  9.     <filter>

  10.         <filter-name>MyServlet2</filter-name>

  12.     </filter>
