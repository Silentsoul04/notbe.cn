JSP简单的练习-功能标签

Nov 15, 2017

<!-- userfn.jsp -->
<%@ page contentType="text/html;charset=gb2312" %>
<%@ taglib prefix="c" uri="http://java.sun.com/jsp/jstl/core" %>
<%@ taglib prefix="fn" uri="http://java.sun.com/jsp/jstl/functions" %>
<html>
<head>
<title>函数使用演示样例</title>
</head>
<body>
	-----------------length函数使用演示样例---------------------<br>
	<c:set var="str" value="**********" />
	字符串"**********"的长度为:${fn:length(str) }<br><br><br>
	
	-----------------字符串大写和小写转换演示样例-------------------<br>
	<c:set var="str2" value="abcdefghijklABCDEFGHIJKL" />
	字符串"abcdefghijklABCDEFGHIJKL"转换为小写:${fn:toLowerCase(str2) }<br>
	字符串"abcdefghijklABCDEFGHIJKL"转换为大写:${fn:toUpperCase(str2) }<br><br><br>
	
	-----------------求子串函数演示样例-------------------------<br>
	字符串"abcdefghijklABCDEFGHIJKL"的下标0到下标4的子串是:${fn:substring(str2,0,4) }<br><br><br>
	
	-----------------replace函数使用演示样例--------------------<br>
	将字符串"abcdefghijklABCDEFGHIJKL"中的"a"替换成"Z":
	<c:set var="frontStr2" value="a" />
	<c:set var="backStr2" value="Z" />
	${fn: replace(str2,frontStr2,backStr2) }<br>
</body>
</html>
