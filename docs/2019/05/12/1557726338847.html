#列表
1、标志：python中，用方括号表示列表，“[]”。
2、基本操作
创建列表：简单的列表是直接输出列表的内容。如下图

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)

append()：在列表的末尾直接添加元素。也可以是往空的列表中添加元素。

motorcycles.append('ducati')
print(motorcycles)

结果为

motorcycles.append('ducati')
print(motorcycles)
['honda', 'yamaha', 'suzuki', 'ducati']

pop()：可删除列表末尾的元素，并能够接着使用它。

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)
popped_motorcycle=motorcycles.pop()
print(motorcycles)
print(popped_motorcycle)

结果如下：

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)
popped_motorcycle=motorcycles.pop()
print(motorcycles)
print(popped_motorcycle)
['honda', 'yamaha', 'suzuki']
['honda', 'yamaha']
suzuki

del()：是删除列表中的指定元素。

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)
del motorcycles[0]
print(motorcycles)

结果为

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)
del motorcycles[0]
print(motorcycles)
['honda', 'yamaha', 'suzuki']
['yamaha', 'suzuki']

拷贝：即一变量为另一变量的完整的切片。

print("My foods are:")
print(my_foods)
print("\nMy friend's foods are:")
print(friend_foods)

3、列表相关方法
（1）读取列表中的某个元素，如：

bicycles=['trek','cannondale','redline','specialized']
print(bicycles)
print(bicycles[0])

输出的是trek。列表的索引是从0开始。
若访问的是最后一个元素，如上例，则为bicycles[-1].
（2）根据值删除元素用remove()函数

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)
motorcycles.append('ducati')
print(motorcycles)
motorcycles.remove('ducati')
print(motorcycles)

结果为

motorcycles=['honda','yamaha','suzuki']
print(motorcycles)
motorcycles.append('ducati')
print(motorcycles)
motorcycles.remove('ducati')
print(motorcycles)
['honda', 'yamaha', 'suzuki']
['honda', 'yamaha', 'suzuki', 'ducati']
['honda', 'yamaha', 'suzuki']

（3）sort()：对数据进行排序

cars=['bmw','audi','toyota','subaru']
cars.sort()
print(cars)

结果

cars=['bmw','audi','toyota','subaru']
cars.sort()
print(cars)
['audi', 'bmw', 'subaru', 'toyota']

sorted()是按照特定顺序显示列表元素，同时不影响它们在列表中的原始排列顺序。

（4）len()：列表的长度。
