在安装过程中遇到报错

gpstate failed. (Reason='Environment Variable MASTER_DATA_DIRECTORY not set!') exiting

需要修改 bashrc.sh 的信息，并且让其生效 source bashrc.sh

source /usr/local/greenplum-db/greenplum_path.sh
export MASTER_DATA_DIRECTORY=/data/master/gpseg-1
