tflearn.models.dnn.DNN与tflearn.DNN

Jan 17, 2018

通过如下几个操作，可以大概看出tflearn.models.dnn.DNN与tflearn.DNN的关系：

print(help(tflearn.models.dnn.DNN))

 
   
 
   
 
 
   
print(help(tflearn.DNN))
 
   

 
   
从返回可以看出，tflearn.models.dnn.DNN与tflearn.DNN是一样的：
